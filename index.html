Pack even more punch in your mission attacks when you master the craft of creating your own weapons. Find useful objects and materials throughout each of the boroughs and create custom weapons tailored to your character.

Raid, Loot & Run from the Law

You create your own destiny in this town. Will you be a theif, a liar, a thug or a hero? The choice is yours, but choose wisely. All choices have consequences – and you and your friends just might pay the ulimate price.

Feeling like you've got survival down to an art? Raise the stakes with the addition of zombie hordes. You'll perfect your technique and level up quickly – if you survive to tell your friends about it.

Choose your friends wisely.

Meet Jake

A loyal and fearless leader, you will make decisions that shape the fate of your whole crew. Jake is smart, resourceful, and tough – and he always has his friends' back. But don't take advantage of his trusting nature. Jake can be ruthless, cunning and devious. Double-crossing him could be the last thing you ever do in this game.
